# 🚀 Deadline Guardian

> AI-powered productivity companion that helps students, professionals, and entrepreneurs plan, prioritize, and complete tasks before deadlines are missed.

## 📌 Problem Statement

**The Last-Minute Life Saver**

Students and professionals often miss deadlines because traditional reminder apps are passive and easy to ignore. Deadline Guardian proactively predicts risks, prioritizes work, and provides AI-powered assistance to ensure tasks are completed on time.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 AI Task Prioritization
- 📅 Smart Calendar Management
- 📊 Productivity Analytics Dashboard
- ⏰ Deadline Risk Prediction
- 💬 AI Assistant for Planning
